About CUSAT Art Gallery

Welcome to CUSAT Art Gallery, a premier online platform dedicated to showcasing artistic expressions from talented creators across the globe. Our digital gallery serves as a vibrant ecosystem where emerging and established artists can display and sell their masterpieces while allowing art enthusiasts to explore and acquire unique paintings, sculptures, and digital art.

Founded in 2023, we've grown from a small university project to one of the most visited online art platforms in the region. Our carefully curated collections represent diverse styles, mediums, and cultural perspectives, ensuring there's something for every art lover.
